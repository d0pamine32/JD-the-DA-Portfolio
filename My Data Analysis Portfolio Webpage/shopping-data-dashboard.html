Customer Shopping Data Dashboard

Microsoft Excel

Have you ever wondered what drives customers to buy certain products or how sales trends change over time? That curiosity led me to this project. I wanted to take raw sales data and turn it into something interactive and insightful. Something that could help businesses understand their customers better and make smarter decisions.

Live Demo. Github Repository

Data Source

The data used in this project was sourced from Kaggle.

.

Data Cleaning

The data was generally well-structured, with the only issue being the invoice_date, which had an inconsistent format and incorrect data type. To resolve this, I standardized the format to MMM/DD/YYYY before converting it to a date data type.

Data Analysis Techniques

Descriptive analysis was used in this project to summarize key shopping trends, including total sales over time and across different locations. The dashboard also provides insights into average spending by age bracket, helping to identify customer purchasing patterns.

Data Visualization

This interactive dashboard provides a detailed analysis of shopping trends through various visualizations. It features time series line charts to monitor sales over time and bar charts for comparing revenue across locations and spending patterns by age group. With dynamic filtering options, users can refine insights based on gender, date, purchased products, or payment methods.

Conclusion

This customer shopping data dashboard provides key insights into sales performance, spending habits, and location-based revenue trends. By analyzing sales by category, total sales per location, and average spending by age group, businesses can better understand consumer behavior and optimize their strategies.

  • Sales per Category
    • Books had the highest sales. Shoes and Technology followed closely behind.
  • Total Sales per Location
    • Mall of Istanbul recorded the highest total sales. Kanyon ranked second, with a small gap in sales.
  • Average Spending by Age Bracket
    • Middle-aged adults had the highest average spending.

Books lead in sales, followed by Shoes and Technology, making them key investment areas. Mall of Istanbul and Kanyon show strong sales, highlighting prime retail locations. Middle-aged adults spend the most, presenting an opportunity for targeted marketing. Businesses should focus on stocking top-selling products, boosting promotions in high-traffic areas, and personalizing offers to maximize revenue.
